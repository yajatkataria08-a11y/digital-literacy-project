### Task 4 — Email Etiquette
> Professional communication standards and social media guidelines

Drafted professional emails for various workplace scenarios and documented best practices for:
- Formal email structure (subject lines, salutations, sign-offs)
- Tone and language in professional contexts
- Social media conduct as a digital professional

> Case study analysis and practical prevention framework

Investigated a real-world online fraud case and produced:
- Detailed breakdown of the attack vector and victim impact
- A practical checklist for individuals to protect against common cybercrimes (phishing, identity theft, social engineering)
